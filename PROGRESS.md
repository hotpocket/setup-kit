## What works

- **`workstation` profile** — manifest + answer-file driven, idempotent,
  doctor(check)/install modes. Tested end-to-end on bare-metal Ubuntu
  24.04 (X11) and 26.04 (Wayland), independently verified by `verify.sh`.
- **`survey`** — read-only hardware probe; safe anywhere, including a live CD.
- **`proxmox-host` scripts** — reviewed but not yet run on a real host.
  `04-create-workstation-vm.sh` is never auto-run (edit its tunables first).
- **Manifests** — generated by `capture/90-generate-manifests.py` from a
  snapshot; re-run on the capture machine after a refresh.

## Two-repo design

- **setup-kit** (this repo) owns machine-level provisioning. `snapshot/` is
  gitignored (large, machine-specific, holds WiFi PSKs).
- **`.configs`** (github.com/hotpocket/.configs) owns user-level
  dotfiles/bin/dconf as its own repo. Phase 06 clones it and runs its
  `setup.sh`. It is currently **private**.
